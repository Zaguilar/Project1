Traveling During a Pandemic

We all know that the COVID 19 Pandemic impacted us all from every angle of our lives. As humans progressed throughout the decades we became more and more intertwined and less separated by distance. Meaning businesses, families, events have become essential to a global economy. As the world slowly opens up again to travel it is important to make informed travel decisions. With our webpage you will be able to search for flights and consider COVID 19 API risks and current status in a foreign country.
